Customer surveys

Hear your customers

Mirror CX allows to perform different types of remote surveys of respondents (CAPI, CATI), and polls (personal interview or tablet survey). You will be able to analyze the survey results together with the results of other types of research (Mystery shopping, employee satisfaction assessment). We are able to deploy a large-scale federal project within one week, including small towns thanks to our database of 5,000 interviewers.

Real “omnichannelity”

Mirror CX can get data from respondents not only through classical web forms with unique links, but in many other ways. This allows you to conduct surveys just as it is convenient to your customers.
