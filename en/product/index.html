3D-analytics for your stores, restaurants, salons and offices

To make your retail business truly successful, you need to talk to 3 people: a customer, a seller and a mystery shopper.

What lies behind the scores and answers?

Track Customer Experience Index in real time. Choose convenient reports forms: in a map, in charts, in tables. Mystery shopping

Manage mystery shopping projects using the database of 95,000 agents throughout the CIS and Eastern Europe. Or delegate management to your contractor and grant him access to Mirror CX. Determine the level of implementation of standards for each of your outlets, form a ranking of managers and sellers, analyze individual reports of mystery shoppers, listen records of audits, see photo reports and videos online.

Customer surveys

Organize automatic surveys of costumers as for satisfaction or create your own surveys. Watch for NPS, CSI and define the main problem areas in the retail network by tips of your customers.

Assessment of employees

Carry out regular assessments of knowledge of employees using a survey. Analyze internal NPS and line personnel satisfaction level.
